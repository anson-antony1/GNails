### Address Common Concerns

**"Do I have to respond to EVERY feedback?"**
> No. Only issues (low ratings) require action. Everything else is recorded for analytics.

**"What if customers don't want texts?"**
> Front desk can skip the phone number. You still get visit record, just no automatic feedback.

**"How much time does this actually take daily?"**
> - Morning: 2-3 minutes checking Today page
> - As issues come in: 5-10 minutes per issue to call customer
> - Weekly: 10 minutes reviewing dashboard trends
> - Total: 30-60 minutes per week, not per day

**"What if technical stuff breaks?"**
> System monitors itself. You get notified if anything fails. Plus there's backup support.
